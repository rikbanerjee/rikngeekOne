# Rik Banerjee
### AI Engineering Leader | Solutions Builder | Innovation Strategist

Rik Banerjee is an AI Engineering Leader with over **18 years of experience** architecting and deploying transformative AI/ML systems at an enterprise scale. Currently serving as the **Lead Director of AI Engineering & Retail Technology at CVS Health**, he leads the AI strategy and execution for a network of over 9,500 retail locations serving 120 million customers.

### Professional Impact
* **Edge AI Pioneer:** Architected and deployed industry-first Computer Vision AI on commodity CPU hardware—delivering significant enterprise-wide ROI without the need for expensive GPU infrastructure.
* **Enterprise AI Builder:** Developed a patent-pending Agentic AI platform that automates 50+ retail workflows.
* **Scalable Leadership:** Successfully scaled engineering organizations in areas of Pickup & Checkout Modernization, Omni-channel Delivery.
* **Strategic Innovator:** Expert at bridging AI research into high-impact production environments, specializing in Edge AI, LLMs, and Agentic automation.

### Education & Credentials
* **MIT:** Executive Education in Applied Agentic AI for Organizational Transformation and the Internet of Things.
* **Harvard Business School:** Executive Education in Competing in the Age of AI.
* **B.Tech:** Electronics & Communication Engineering.
* **Intellectual Property:** Holds multiple patents and pending applications in Computer Vision, Agentic AI, and Biometric Authentication.

### Community & Volunteering
Based in **Westborough, MA**, Rik is deeply committed to technical mentorship and the local community.

* **Mentorship:** Serves as a mentor for the PTECH program at Woonsocket High School and volunteers for CS4RI programming initiatives.
* **Technical Advisor:** Acts as a Volunteer and Technical Advisor for **The CustomHUB LLC**, an e-commerce marketplace dedicated to Indian-themed apparel.
* **Builder at Heart:** An avid hobbyist in the AI space, Rik continuously prototypes new AI applications and experiences to push the boundaries of what is possible.
